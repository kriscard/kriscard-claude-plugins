# Deep Analysis

Perform multi-dimensional analysis of: $ARGUMENTS

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Problem Definition
- What is the core issue?
- Who is affected?
- What are the symptoms vs root causes?

### 2. Context Mapping
- What factors influence this?
- What constraints exist?
- What has been tried before?

### 3. Stakeholder Analysis
- Who has interest in the outcome?
- What are their perspectives?
- Where do interests align or conflict?

### 4. Options Generation
- What are possible approaches?
- What are the trade-offs?
- What are the risks of each?

### 5. Evaluation Criteria
- How will success be measured?
- What are the priorities?
- What are acceptable trade-offs?

### 6. Recommendation
- What is the recommended approach?
- What are the next steps?
- What should be monitored?

## Output Format

Provide analysis with:
- Clear structure by dimension
- Evidence for conclusions
- Explicit assumptions
- Actionable recommendations
